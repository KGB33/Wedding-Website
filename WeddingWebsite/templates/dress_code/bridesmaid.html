Dress Code -- Bridesmaids

I don’t care if you spend $5 or $500 dollars on your dress, find something comfortable that you’ll be able to wear again! Please keep in mind that we will be at a high altitude so it will likely be in the 50s outside. Consider a heavy fabric, longer sleeves or get a cardigan/shawl/wrap to go along with your dress. Shoes: pick something comfortable that you can walk outside/on the grass with. I don’t care what color, just don’t be dumb and wear like bright orange tennis shoes. Jewelry/Hair/Other: Do what you want, keep it classy. Please let me know if you have any questions! If you find a dress you like but aren’t sure about it I’m happy to look at it, but I don’t need to approve everyone’s dresses, I trust you guys.
